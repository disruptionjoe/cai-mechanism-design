# Roadmap

1. Receive one source-backed inquiry that lacks a better existing owner.
2. Search competing mechanisms and explicit no-action alternatives.
3. Design synthetic or least-consequential tests with stops and affected-party standing.
4. Record evidence and choose kill, revise, defer, transfer, or propose graduation.

Do not create prototypes, institutions, incentive systems, or spin-outs merely
to make the incubator look active.
